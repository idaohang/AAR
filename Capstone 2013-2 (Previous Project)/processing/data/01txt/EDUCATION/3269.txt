This chapter explores the following features of Vietnamese history:  Vietnamese struggle against French colonialism, the developoment and nature of Vietnamese nationalism, Ho Chi Minh's leadership, the impact of the Second World War and the Japanese occupation, the war against the French 1945-54. The chapter incorporates a narrative commentary of key events and movements with a range of carefully selected and researched historical documents. Questions, activities, value dilemmas and suggestions for furhter research encourage students to think critically about the sources presented.
