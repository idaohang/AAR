Research and practices in electronic business over wireless devices (m-business) have recently seen an exponential growth. The applications and services that were envisioned for m-business are becoming a reality today. This paper presents the basic concepts necessary to understand m-business applications. This paper also presents acase study of the voice driven airline ticketing system that can be accessed at anytime, anywhere by mobile phones. The specific goals for this system are to allow users to search for flight information and purchase an airline ticket to a destination. The application offers maximum functionality while still maintaining high level of user convenience in terms of input and navigation.
