This booklet was produced to promote educational use of Museums and cultural centres and to support history teachers in the Pacific Islands. It was delivered free of chrage to schools, institutions in the region. It was wublished by HistoryCOPs, a regional professional development project funded since 1995 by the Sasakawa Pacific Islands Nations Fund, Japan. The manuscript was prepared at the Vanuatu Social Studies and History Teachers Association (VSSTA) workshop, held at the Vanuatu Cultural Centre and Museum, Port Vila, Oct 23-26 2001. The publication was endorsed by   (PIMA)Pacific Islands Museum Association and supported by the Vanuatu Education Department, the VSSTA and HistoryCOPs.  This material may be copied freely, with appropriate acknowledgement made to HistoryCOPs.
