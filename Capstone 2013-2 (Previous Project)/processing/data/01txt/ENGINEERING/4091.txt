The annual QUT Courses and Careers Day was recently held at the Gardens Point campus with around 15,000 visitors. Congratulations to our 2001 Outstanding Alumni Award Winners, who were announced at the QUT Alumni Awards breakfast on Wednesday 1 August.  The Faculty of Built Environment and Engineering Winner was prominent Brisbane surveyor and businessman Jim McNoulty - Chairman, Pike Mirls McKnoulty. Jim has emerged as a leading promoter of the all-important area of sustainable development in the community
