Research Focus on the physical land and water resources in the Linthorpe Catchment and their impacts in that area. Study of the relationship of property boundaries to the land types in the catchement study area. Design, data gathering, field data collection for developing a geographic information system(GIS) in the catchment. GIS query and sample outputs in the form of mapping, text reports and graphers are presented. How QUT has been applying Global Positioning Technology (GPS) to collection data layers in Linthorpe.
