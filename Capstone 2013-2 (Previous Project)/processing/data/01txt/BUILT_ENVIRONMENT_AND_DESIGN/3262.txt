The characteristics of effective teamwork in construction are examined.  From the general management literature, the most common of these are expected to be clear objectives, trust, cohesiveness, interdependency and enthusiasm of the team members.  This is confirmed by an empirical questionnaire survey of a sample of 57 Singapore construction industry personnel where, in terms of correlation, the highest characteristics are trust and cohesiveness, followed by clear objectives, enthusiasm and interdependency.  In terms of respondentsâ€™ views, the most necessary was clear objectives, followed by interdependency, enthusiasm, trust and cohesiveness.
