The purpose of this final report is to consolidate a number of individual project surveys into one finalised document. This project has involved the provision of survey services for the client in terms of providing 3 dimensional coordinate positional information on boreholes in the various investigation areas. This surveying project was conducted by Mr Robert Webb, School of Urban Development, Faculty of Built Environment and Engineering, QUT. The client for this project was Dr Malcolm E Cox, School of Natural Resource Sciences, Faculty of Science, QUT.  This report is organised around the four project survey areas; namely  Black Swamp; Toorbul; Meldale and Ningi investigation areas West of the Pumistone Passage.
